About Us

Our team’s strength lies in its interdisciplinary nature, with members from various academic backgrounds, which has proven crucial in validating the innovation. During our charity activities at the nursing home, we discovered that many individuals are unable to communicate normally with their families and healthcare staff due to throat damage. This inspired our initial idea.

We have a clear division of responsibility in group work. Jiaxin, who has experience working with speech-impaired communities, provided us with essential insights into user needs and refined the user-centric features. Xifang, with a background in biology, helped the team understand the relationship between laryngeal muscles, the nervous system, and the mechanism of vocalization. Meanwhile, Ruichen with a background in economics evaluated the market landscape, ensuring that the product would be affordable and appealing to the market. Lastly, Zhiru with expertise in mechanical engineering offered innovative ideas for the design and functionality of the device.

Our Vision

To empower individuals with speech impairments by providing innovative, accessible, and effective communication solutions that enhance their quality of life and promote social inclusion.

Our Mission

Our mission is to develop a groundbreaking, non-invasive device that translates electrocardiography signals from the throat into clear speech, enabling individuals with throat-related medical conditions to communicate effectively and regain their confidence. We aim to create a world where everyone, regardless of their speech ability, can participate fully in society.
